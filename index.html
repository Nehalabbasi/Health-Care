Unique Features

ACXSS provides transparency into a cumbersome and chaotic supply chain process, offering an intelligent, efficient, and modern solution. Our goal is to significantly improve every step of the procurement process.

Order Supplies

The ACXSS Marketplace is a cloud-based, easy to use multi-vendor ordering platform for multiple users and locations.

Personalized Dashboard

The ACXSS Dashboard provides instant oversight into your entire organization with real-time analytics, reporting, and true spend visibility.

Requestor/Approver

The Requestor/Approver feature allows the administration to set specific approval thresholds for orders by user, spend, and location.

Procure To Pay

Our Procure to Pay process allows you to efficiently manage the entire purchase order life cycle.

Virtual Materials Manager

The ACXSS Virtual Materials Manager provides master item file management & maintenance including GPO and contract alignment.

Three Way Matching

Our Three Way Matching tool allows for confirmations between the purchase order, the packing slip, and the invoice.

Customized Reporting

Our Customized Reporting allows for real-time spend visibility across all locations with the enterprise to line-item detail.

Smart Inventory

Our Smart Inventory tool allows you to enhance your supply chain process by establishing a rules-based inventory ordering system.
