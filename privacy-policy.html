Cookies
This Site serves "cookies" to help You navigate about the Site and to track the traffic to Our Site. You have the option to refuse the cookies but may experience some service interruptions as a result; for example, online communities will not operate properly. The Website automatically places a text file called a "cookie" in the browser files of Your computer. Cookies represent bits of information that a Website may transfer to a visitor's hard disk for the purpose of record keeping. Cookies help Us evaluate Your use of Our Website, such as the kinds of information You want to see and the kinds You most likely never will want to read. Cookie technology may also enable Websites such as Ours to ask for Your registration and preferences only once. During subsequent visits, You will not necessarily be required to sign in again. Cookies let Us avoid showing You the same advertising messages repeatedly and do not contain or retain personal information. However, they do let us relate Your use of the Site to information that You have specifically and knowingly provided to ACXSS.
